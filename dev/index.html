NEOs.jl

[] (https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.5152449) [] (https://github.com/PerezHz/NEOs.jl/actions/workflows/CI.yml) [] (https://coveralls.io/github/PerezHz/NEOs.jl?branch=main) [] (https://codecov.io/gh/PerezHz/NEOs.jl)

A Julia package for high-accuracy propagation, orbit determination and impact monitoring of Near-Earth Objects. It exploits jet transport techniques via TaylorSeries.jl and [TaylorIntegration.jl] (https://github.com/PerezHz/TaylorIntegration.jl).

Authors

  • Jorge A. Pérez-Hernández, Minor Planet Center, Harvard & Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics
  • Luis Benet, Instituto de Ciencias Físicas, Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México (UNAM)
  • Luis E. Ramírez Montoya, Instituto de Ciencias Físicas, Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México (UNAM)

Installation

NEOs is a registered package, and is simply installed by running

pkg> add NEOs
Julia 1.10 or later is required

NEOs requires Julia 1.10 or later.

Tested Julia versions

NEOs is currently tested on Julia 1.10 and 1.12.
